### New Open Dataset Published: Interactive dashboard: Mindfulness-based interventions and anxiety reduction in Latin America. DOI: 10.7910/DVN/BR5HUF

**Universidad Internacional de La Rioja (UNIR)** — Juan Moisés de la Serna Tuya (ORCID: https://orcid.org/0000-0002-8401-8018) has published a new open dataset: *Interactive dashboard: Mindfulness-based interventions and anxiety reduction in Latin America. DOI: 10.7910/DVN/BR5HUF*.
